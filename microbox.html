MicroBox and Biofeedback


ARTIST STATEMENT



Make a 'micro synth' biofeedback device that can 'sense' various inputs and then send out control signals like sound and light. in Addition you will use it to create a device with a form of Biofeedback. To do this you must use the big 4 schematics: (digital in, digital out, analog in and analog out) to create a synth that both demonstrates these fundemental hardware interface relationships but also embraces how machines create their character, identity and meaning. The device will be placed in a enclosure of your own design.
