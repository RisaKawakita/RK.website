GSRs

Part A. Basics Biofeedback

Use the basic GSR program or a variation to do a series of several bio feedback sessions. Take 5-10 minutes each day to use your micro box as your own bio feedback syste. Try and control/influence your own stress levels. Can your mind influence the situation? The goal is to practice a little each day, to see what the different activity levels and how much you are able to use it to influence or change your own stress levels. This can be positive or negative. See the reference material above for more info on the GSR used in bio feedback medicine. After each sessiion, keep a breif log on how each session went.
