BrushBots


ARTIST STATEMENT



Create a Brushbot based on the parts and diagram provided below. Use both soldering and (hot) glue to fasten and connect the eletronics to the 'brush'. When finished you should be able to balance the bot, with the wires and you can also add any additional appendages, for style , balance, etc. Details on how to connect up the different parts will be explained in class. If you need some tips on how to solder check the examples provided on the Resource page. Be sure to make solid solder connections, so that when its starts to vibrate it does not shake itself loose. Lastly dont forget to have fun spruce up your brushbot into something that is fun to look at and play with. Bedazzle your bots with themes, characters, behaviors, etc.
