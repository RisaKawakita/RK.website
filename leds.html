LEDs


ARTIST STATEMENT



Create a wearable led circuit (hair ball wearable) with serial and parallel circuits. You will also be required to make your own custom foam switch. In this project, I made LEDs on a mask as a wearable LED circuit, where when I have a bad posture while wearing this mask on, the part of the circuit placed on the chin will hit on another circuit part and will turn the LED lights on.
