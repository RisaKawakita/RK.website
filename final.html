This is my Final Project for ART 106, Human-machine-interaction. This GSR device is connected to my Laptop, and it receives a code from the laptop. Here with using this device, you can measure your amount of strees by touching the sensor on the device. It can detact your stress level from your skin, the detail such as sweat, heartrate, and your skin texture as well.

In this project, I use this device to measure my stress level for myself to determine what kind of food I should get when I am under high levels of stress. First I measure my current stress amount when I am thinking about all the final exams and projects that are coming, then I tested my stress level again while I am eating my favotite food. Although those are all my favorite, they have totally different taste which is spicy, sweet and sour.
